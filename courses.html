Certification Requirements

Initial Certification: Complete the appropriate initial training course. You'll receive a certificate valid for one year.

Annual Refresher: All asbestos certifications must be renewed annually. You must complete refresher training before your certification expires.

WA L&I Registration: After training, you must register with Washington State Labor & Industries to receive your official certification card. We provide assistance with this.
