Meet The Instructor

Michael Thomas

I've been working in environmental consulting for over 20 years.

Over that time, I've conducted thousands of asbestos inspections, supervised abatement projects, and trained workers across Washington, Idaho, and the Pacific Northwest.

I started MICRIST Environmental because I saw a gap in the Inland Northwest: quality asbestos training that goes beyond reading slides. When you take my class, you get real-world knowledge from someone who has crawled through attics, collected bulk samples, and written inspection reports.

This isn't theory from a corporate manual. It's practical knowledge from someone who does the work.

Credentials

  • 20+ Years Environmental Consulting Experience
  • Washington State L&I Approved Instructor
  • EPA AHERA Accredited Inspector & Trainer
  • Based in Spokane, serving the entire region

Why "MICRIST"?

MICRIST stands for Michael I Christopher Richard Scott Thomas—my full name.

It's a bit unusual, and honestly, people misspell it all the time. That's why our website is SpokaneAsbestosTraining.com—easier to remember.

But the name represents something important: this is a personal business. When you train with MICRIST Environmental, you're training with me directly. Not a corporate franchise, not a rotating cast of instructors. Just experience, passed directly to you.
