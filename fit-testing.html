When is Fit Testing Required?

OSHA regulations (29 CFR 1910.134) mandate fit testing in these situations:

  • Before first use of a respirator
  • Annually thereafter (every 12 months)
  • Whenever a different size, style, or brand is used
  • After significant facial changes (weight loss, surgery, etc.)
